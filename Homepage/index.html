    Frequently Asked Questions

    Got questions? We've got answers!

    To become a blood donor, you need to sign up on our platform, complete your profile, and schedule an appointment for donation.

    The eligibility criteria for blood donation include being in good health, being at least 17 years old, and weighing at least 50 kg. Certain medical conditions and travel history may also affect eligibility.

    You can donate whole blood every 8 weeks, or plasma and platelets more frequently. It's important to check with your local blood bank for specific guidelines.

    Yes, blood donation is safe. We follow strict protocols to ensure the safety of donors and recipients. Single-use, sterile needles are used for each donation, and donors are carefully screened to ensure they are healthy enough to donate.

    We prioritize your privacy and data security. Our platform uses encryption and other security measures to protect your personal information.

    To request blood, sign up on our platform and submit a request with the necessary details. We will match you with available donors and ensure a timely and efficient process.
