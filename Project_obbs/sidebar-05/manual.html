User Manual

A step-by-step guide on how to use our website

Registering as Donor

1. Click on the Register button on the side navigation.
2. Enter your name.
3. Enter your Identification Number according to the following format.
4. Enter your phone number according to the following format.
5. Enter your current address.
6. Pick your marital status.
7. Click on the agreement button.
8. Press register. Your registration will be processed right after.
9. Once done, you can see your profile by clicking "Profile" in the navigations bar. Once filled it will look as below.

Booking An Appointment

1. Click on the Appointment button on the side navigation.
2. Click the blue button labeled "Book an appointment".
3. Pick your prefered time and date from the available slots shown in the pop-up.
4. Enter the required credentials and a coonfirmation pop-up will appear.
